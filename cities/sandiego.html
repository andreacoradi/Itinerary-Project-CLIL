San Diego

General Information

San Diego is a city sited in the West Coast. The population is 1.42 milion people who lives in an area of 964.5 km squared. The average temperature high is 25 degrees celsius an the average low is 19 degrees.

Points of Interests

The main points of interests are the Balboa park which is a wide green area that hosts the San Diego Museum of Art and the San Diego Zoo. Another POI is Little Italy, a neighbourhood in Downtown San Diego which is full of Italian restaurants and grocery stores, home design stores, art galleries and residential units. Old Town is another neighbourhood which contains Old Town San Diego State Historic Park and Presidio Park, both of which are listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

Los Angeles

General Information

Los Angeles is a city sited in the West Coast. The population is 4 milion people who lives in an area of 1,302 km squared. The average temperature high is 29 degrees celsius an the average low is 16 degrees.

Points of Interests

Venice Beach is one of the most important turistic place, in fact there are a lot of shops that face the sea. The Hollywood neighbourhood is famous all around the world for beign the most important cinematic city where a lot of actors and directors live. The Santa Monica Luna Park is also a very important park.

Nightlife

Silver Lake, Los Feliz, Downtown and Hollywood are the coolest neighbourhood in Los Angeles for the nightlife. There are a lot of cinemas and bars with music and very nice drinks.

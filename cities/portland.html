Portland

General Information

Portland is a city sited in the West Coast. The population is 647, 805 people who lives in an area of 375.5 km squared. The average temperature high is 18 degrees celsius an the average low is 5 degrees.

Points of Interests

The Oregon Zoo hosts a lot of endangered animals like elephants and proposes a lot of shows. The Lan Su Chinese Garden is a botanic oasis with a traditional tea room. Another important POI is the Portland Art Museum that expose Andy Warhol art and operas.

Nightlife

Cocktail bars, craft beer bars, tiki bars, old school strip clubs - Portland's nightlife scene offers a variety of bars and clubs for a night out on the town.
